## Design Consistency Across All Screens

### Colors
- **Brand Blue**: `#8F8CFF` - Primary CTAs, active states, progress
- **Text**: `#111111` (primary), `#52525B` (secondary), `#71717A` (tertiary)
- **Backgrounds**: `#FAFAFA` (page), `#FFFFFF` (cards)
- **Borders**: `#E4E4E7` (default), `#D4D4D8` (hover)

### Typography
- **Large Headings**: 40-56px Instrument Serif
- **Section Titles**: 22px Inter Semibold
- **Card Titles**: 16-18px Inter Semibold
- **Body Text**: 14-15px Inter Regular

### Spacing
- **Section Gaps**: 96px (mb-24)
- **Card Gaps**: 20px (gap-5)
- **Card Padding**: 24px (p-6)
- **Button Padding**: 16px vertical, 24px horizontal

### Corner Radius
- **Buttons**: 10px
- **Cards**: 12px
- **Large Cards**: 14px
- **Modals**: 16px

### Transitions
- **Duration**: 200ms
- **Easing**: ease-in-out
- **Hover Effects**: -translate-y-1, shadow-lg

## User Journey Summary

1. **Home** → Click "Record Screen"
2. **Modal** → Configure settings → "Start Recording"
3. **Recording** → Record content → "Stop Recording"
4. **Processing** → AI analyzes (auto-advance)
5. **Intent** → Choose outcome (key decision) ⭐
6. **Recommendations** → Review AI insights → "Continue"
7. **Preview** → Review generated content → "Publish"
8. **Success** → Share or return home

**Total Time**: < 5 minutes
**User Decisions**: 3 (recording settings, outcome type, publish/edit)
**AI Automation**: 90% of the work
