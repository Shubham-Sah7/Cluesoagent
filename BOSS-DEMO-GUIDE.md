## 🎨 Design Quality Points

### Throughout Both Demos, Point Out:

1. **Reduced Roundness**
   - Modal: 16px (not 24px)
   - Cards: 12px (not 20px)
   - Buttons: 10px (not 16px)
   - No pill shapes

2. **No Decorative Elements**
   - No sparkle icons
   - No AI badges
   - No large floating icons
   - No unnecessary illustrations

3. **Professional Language**
   - "Upload Complete" not "Uploading..."
   - "Transcript Ready" not "AI is working its magic"
   - "Processing recording" not "AI is generating"

4. **Strategic Color Use**
   - Brand color (#8F8CFF) only for:
     - Selected states
     - Primary CTAs
     - Progress indicators
     - Focus states
   - Not dominating the screen

5. **Standard Button Sizes**
   - 160-200px max width
   - Never full-width
   - Proper proportions

6. **Better Density**
   - Tighter layouts
   - Intentional spacing
   - Better rhythm
   - More content visible

7. **Typography Refinement**
   - Medium weight headings
   - Regular body text
   - Consistent spacing
   - Reduced sizes
